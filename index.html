Step-by-step reminder

1
Fill each cell: Multiply the digit at the top of each column by the digit at the right of each row. Write the tens in the top-left triangle and units in the bottom-right.
2
Add the diagonals: Follow the color paths! Starting from the bottom-right, add all numbers sitting inside the same colored stripes. Type your sum in the matching colored box at the bottom.
3
Carry if needed: If a diagonal sum is 10 or more, write down the units digit and carry the tens digit into your addition for the next colored diagonal path to its left.
4
Read your answer: Read your verified diagonal sums from left to right — that's your final answer! Enter it above to check.
