Step-by-step reminder

1
Fill each cell: Multiply the digit at the top of each column by the digit at the right of each row. Write the tens in the top-left triangle and units in the bottom-right.
2
Add the diagonals: Follow the color paths! Starting from the bottom-right, add all numbers sitting inside the same colored stripes. The totals appear below.
3
Carry if needed: If a diagonal sum is 10 or more, carry the tens digit into the next colored diagonal path to its left.
4
Read your answer: Read the diagonal sums from left to right — that's your final answer! Enter it above to check.
